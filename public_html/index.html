About The Event

The Ideathon is a platform that brings together innovative minds from diverse backgrounds to collaborate and turn their ideas into reality. The event is a unique opportunity to network with like-minded individuals and create solutions that can have a lasting impact on the world. Our goal is to foster a creative environment where individuals can share their ideas and bring them to life through collaboration and teamwork. The Ideathon will feature "Ideation, team building, MVP, Business model canvas, and pitching. Further, we have keynote speakers, interactive workshops, and opportunities for attendees to showcase their ideas and receive feedback from industry experts. We are excited to bring together individuals from a wide range of backgrounds, including students, entrepreneurs, scientists, and more, to collaborate and create innovative solutions. Whether you have a fully formed idea or just a spark of inspiration, we invite you to join us and be a part of the Ideathon event. So come with an open mind, a willingness to learn, and a passion for innovation. We look forward to seeing the amazing ideas that will be born at this year's Ideathon.

Amity University Rajasthan

Welcome to the world of Amity University, where knowledge, discipline and recreation come together! The center of learning set amidst a lush green valley is a treat for the eyes and the mind. Amity University, Rajasthan (AUR), offers its students a campus for their superlative development. Amity University Rajasthan, Jaipur was established in the State of Rajasthan, by an Act of the State Legislature, passed on March 29, 2008, under the patronage of RBEF, a registered, non-profit society. AUR is recognized by UGC under Section 2f and empowered to award degrees under section 22. Amity University Rajasthan has a beautiful eco-friendly green campus spread over 152 acres of landmass. The University takes pride in its world-class infrastructural facilities which have been scientifically and aesthetically designed for the optimal use of natural resources. Individuals from many different walks of life have been awarded Honorary Degrees by Amity University. The recipient includes world leaders and royal figures, eminent scientists, engineers, and inventors, individuals who have made significant contributions in branches of business and industry, those who have distinguished themselves in many fields across the creative and performing arts and sports, and others who in their individual way, have made a significant contribution to the greater good of society, or to the life of the University. Some of the distinguished recipients of Honoris Causa include Dr. Vijay P. Bhatkar, Shri N. R. Narayana Murthy, Lord Jeffery Archer, Shri G.E. Vahanvati, Noble Laurette Prof. Muhammad Yunus, Padmashree, Dr. D.R. Mehta, Mr. Prasoon Joshi among others

Entrepreneurship-Cell, AUR

Entrepreneurship Cell AUR aims to promote the entrepreneurship culture in and around the campus by providing multiple platforms to budding entrepreneurs where we can improve upon our business models, get mentorship from successful entrepreneurs, interact with investors, and groom our entrepreneurship skills. Basically, we provide a conducive and healthy environment for budding entrepreneurs. The Entrepreneurship Cell, AUR is also the primary entrepreneurshippromoting body of the Amity University Rajasthan managed and run completely by the students and faculties of the institute. The E-Cell AUR is constantly hustling to support the hustlers of our country. It has constantly been working to give entrepreneurs a platform where they can get support and nurture their ideas.
